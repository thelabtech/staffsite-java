Sharing of Information

Campus Crusade for Christ will not share any visitor information (including email addresses) with any third party.


Purchases, Donations, and Registrations

Campus Crusade's site uses a Secured Socket Layer (SSL) server for its order forms and for ministry partners to make donations and purchases online. For this, users need to provide contact and financial information. Information collected from a person may be used to verify his or her qualifications for registration and to bill them for products and services.


Third Parties

This site contains links to other Web sites. The ministry is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such Web sites.


Security

This site has security measures in place to prevent the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under the ministry's control.
