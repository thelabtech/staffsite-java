Why do so many kids enter college and lose the fervor they once had for Christ?

If you are a high school student, you've probably never thought about it. But if you are in youth ministry, the odds are that you have. The fact of the matter is that sometimes kids just need someone to help them find an environment where they can continue to grow spiritually.

LINCZone wants to help in making the spiritual transition to life on the college campus. LINCZone will connect you (or your students if you are a youth minister) with to the ministries you request information from. Simply submit the information below and it will be made available to evangelical campus ministries on the student's destination campus.

If you are a student, get started on the right foot in college. If you are a youth pastor, ensure that your investment in your students' lives will continue to pay off.
